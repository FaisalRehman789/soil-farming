About Soil Farming Agent

This application helps farmers and agricultural professionals understand which crops grow best in different soil types and connects them with seed and crop distributors.

Key features:

  • Detailed information about various soil types
  • Crop recommendations based on soil characteristics
  • Directory of seed and crop distributors
  • Search functionality to find suitable crops for your soil
